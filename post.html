Glimps of India

The bustling city of Delhi, often referred to as the "Heart of India," is a city that seamlessly blends its ancient heritage with modernity, offering a rich tapestry of experiences to its travelers. Explore the bustling markets of Chandi Chowk, and take a dive into the grandeur past of India through the lens of the historical marvels. Delhi serves as a gateway to explore the vibrant culture of north India, including Rajasthan and Agra. The city of Agra is known for the Magnificent Taj. The mausoleum was built by Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. The third Destination, Jaipur, showcases the royal heritage of Rajasthan, the pink city boasts a treasure trove of the artistic prowess of the Rajput kings and leaves one mesmerized with its music, dance and cuisine.
